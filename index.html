Hi there, thanks for visiting my website! I am a junior undergraduate student majoring in Software Engineering at Xi'an Jiaotong University.

I am currently interning at the LUD Undergraduate Research Group at Xi'an Jiaotong University, under the supervision of Professor Minnan Luo. LUD is entirely founded and run by a group of undergraduates interested in NLP. Dozens of senior students from the group have already published research papers as first authors during their undergraduate studies.

My primary research interest lies in building a healthier and more transparent internet information ecosystem in the age of artificial intelligence. Currently, my research focuses on two main directions: more effectively detecting harmful information such as fake news, and mining the underlying intent behind content like sarcasm and metaphors on social media.
